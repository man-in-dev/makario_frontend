Q: Will my data be saved if I refresh the page?
A: Currently NO. Data is stored in browser memory.
   To save data permanently, you'll need to connect to a database.
   See ADMIN_CRUD_IMPLEMENTATION.md for details.

Q: Can I undo a deletion?
A: NO. Deletions are permanent. That's why there's a confirmation dialog.
   Be careful! We recommend backing up data first.

Q: Can I bulk delete multiple items?
A: NOT YET. Future feature coming soon.
   Currently, you must delete one item at a time.

Q: Why is the export button only on Orders?
A: Other pages will get export feature soon.
   Orders page had it as priority.

Q: Can I sort the table by clicking column headers?
A: NOT YET. Future feature.
   Currently, filtering and searching are available.

Q: What happens if I don't fill required fields?
A: The form shows an error message and prevents submission.
   Look for fields marked with * (asterisk).

Q: Can I edit multiple items at once?
A: NOT YET. Future feature.
   Currently, you must edit one item at a time.
